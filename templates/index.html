Introducing mian

A new way to visualize and analyse the microbiome in your browser. Built by the research community for the research community.



  SIGNUP  

mian features

A mian account gets you access to tools that will help you visually explore your microbiome dataset. Best of all, it's completely free.

Cloud Enabled

Any data that you work with is accessible anytime anywhere.

Responsive Filtering

Pick and choose to use different levels of filtering of your data.

Powerful Visualizations

Easily see new trends and patterns in the form of bar charts, scatterplots, and more.

Statistical Analysis

Instantly see the statistical significance of any visualizations.
